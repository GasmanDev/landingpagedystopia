INTRODUCTION

"San Andreas: The Incident" is a story-driven sandbox-survival RPG gamemode developed for GTA San Andreas using the Multi Theft Auto platform. It's events are placed in an alternative time-line, and take place after a catastrophic earthquake that separated San Andreas from mainland North America.

It is still largely a work in progress, and I am looking for like-minded developers to help me complete it.

STORY

The earthquake of '88 was just a taste of what was to follow. After sitting dormant for many years, the San Andreas Fault finally awaken in 2003. The much feared catastrophic earthquake smashed the cities and countryside alike, and the whole state of San Andreas was left separated from the mainland. Death and mayhem roamed free, and the world descended into chaos. What the government did not tell the survivors was that, while the National Guard and the emergency services struggled to contain the disaster and minimize the collateral damage, an even greater evil was silently taking over.
